Dilushi Piumwardane

About Me

I received my PhD degree from Uppsala University in June 2024. The main focus of my thesis was on solving practical problems that occur when deploying multiple backscatter tags. I have more than seven years of experience working with low power communication technologies. I like to creatively solve real world problems.
